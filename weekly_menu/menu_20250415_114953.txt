Here's a balanced weekly vegetarian menu with a variety of ingredients for breakfast, lunch, and dinner:

Day 1:
Breakfast: Yogurt parfait with granola, mixed berries, and a drizzle of honey
Lunch: Quinoa salad with roasted vegetables, feta cheese, and balsamic dressing
Dinner: Stuffed bell peppers with rice, black beans, and tomato sauce

Day 2:
Breakfast: Whole-grain toast with avocado, sliced tomato, and a sprinkle of salt and pepper
Lunch: Vegetable wrap with hummus, lettuce, cucumber, and carrots
Dinner: Vegetarian chili with cornbread

Day 3:
Breakfast: Smoothie bowl with spinach, banana, almond milk, and topped with chia seeds and sliced almonds
Lunch: Caprese sandwich with fresh mozzarella, tomato, basil, and balsamic glaze on ciabatta bread
Dinner: Vegetable stir-fry with tofu, broccoli, mushrooms, and snow peas over brown rice

Day 4:
Breakfast: Oatmeal with diced apples, cinnamon, and a dollop of almond butter
Lunch: Lentil soup with a side salad and whole-grain crackers
Dinner: Eggplant parmesan with a side of roasted asparagus

Day 5:
Breakfast: Breakfast burrito with scrambled eggs, black beans, salsa, and avocado
Lunch: Greek salad with romaine lettuce, cucumber, tomato, red onion, Kalamata olives, and feta cheese
Dinner: Vegetarian lasagna with spinach and ricotta cheese

Day 6:
Breakfast: Banana pancakes with a side of fresh fruit
Lunch: Tomato and basil soup with a grilled cheese sandwich on whole-grain bread
Dinner: Vegetable curry with chickpeas, cauliflower, and sweet potatoes over basmati rice

Day 7:
Breakfast: Shakshuka (baked eggs in tomato sauce) with a side of whole-grain toast
Lunch: Veggie burger with a whole-grain bun, lettuce, tomato, and avocado
Dinner: Portobello mushroom steaks with roasted potatoes and green beans

This menu provides a balanced mix of proteins, carbohydrates, and healthy fats from various vegetarian sources, ensuring a nutritious and diverse diet throughout the week.
